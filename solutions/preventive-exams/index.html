Preventive Health Exams

Don't wait for symptoms. Stay ahead with comprehensive men's health screenings.

Why Preventive Care Matters

Men are significantly less likely than women to visit a doctor for routine health care. This leads to later diagnoses, worse outcomes, and preventable health crises. Our preventive health exams are designed to change that by making comprehensive screening easy, thorough, and focused on what matters most for men.

What's Included

  • Complete Bloodwork: CBC, metabolic panel, lipid panel, liver and kidney function, glucose/A1C
  • Hormone Panel: Testosterone (total and free), thyroid, DHEA, cortisol, estradiol
  • Cardiovascular Markers: Advanced lipid testing, inflammation markers (CRP, homocysteine)
  • Metabolic Health: Insulin, blood sugar, body composition assessment
  • Prostate Screening: PSA testing (age-appropriate)
  • Vitamin & Nutrient Status: Vitamin D, B12, iron, magnesium
  • Physical Examination: Blood pressure, heart, lungs, and overall health assessment

How Often Should You Get Checked?

  • Under 40: Annual bloodwork, comprehensive exam every 1-2 years
  • 40-50: Annual comprehensive exam with full bloodwork
  • Over 50: Annual comprehensive exam with expanded screening panels
