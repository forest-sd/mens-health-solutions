What Is Testosterone Replacement Therapy?

Testosterone Replacement Therapy (TRT) is a medical treatment designed to restore testosterone levels in men whose bodies no longer produce enough on their own. Low testosterone — or "Low T" — affects millions of men and can significantly impact quality of life.

Signs of Low Testosterone

  • Persistent fatigue and low energy despite adequate sleep
  • Decreased muscle mass and increased body fat
  • Low libido and reduced sexual performance
  • Brain fog, difficulty concentrating, and memory issues
  • Mood changes — irritability, depression, or anxiety
  • Loss of motivation and drive
  • Decreased bone density

Our Approach

At Men's Health Solutions, TRT isn't one-size-fits-all. We begin with comprehensive bloodwork and a thorough evaluation of your symptoms, health history, and goals. Your treatment plan is customized and closely monitored with regular lab work and follow-up appointments to ensure optimal, safe results.

Treatment Options

  • Injectable Testosterone: Weekly or bi-weekly injections — the most common and cost-effective method
  • Testosterone Pellets: Implanted subcutaneously every 3-6 months for steady release
  • Topical Testosterone: Gels or creams applied daily for consistent levels

What to Expect

Most men begin noticing improvements within 3-6 weeks — increased energy, better mood, improved mental clarity, and enhanced libido. Full benefits typically develop over 3-6 months as hormone levels stabilize.
