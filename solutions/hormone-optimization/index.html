Beyond Testosterone

While testosterone gets the most attention, optimal male health depends on the balance of multiple hormones. At Men's Health Solutions, our hormone optimization programs evaluate and address the full picture — because true vitality requires more than just one number on a lab report.

Hormones We Evaluate

  • Testosterone (total and free): The primary male hormone affecting energy, muscle, mood, and libido
  • Estradiol: Excess estrogen in men causes weight gain, fatigue, and mood issues
  • Thyroid (TSH, T3, T4): Directly impacts metabolism, energy, and weight
  • Cortisol: Chronic stress hormone elevation affects every system in the body
  • DHEA: A precursor hormone involved in immune function and vitality
  • Insulin: Metabolic health marker critical for weight and energy management
  • Growth Hormone/IGF-1: Affects body composition, recovery, and aging

Our Process

  1. Comprehensive bloodwork and symptom evaluation
  2. Personalized optimization plan based on your results and goals
  3. Regular monitoring and protocol adjustments
  4. Lifestyle integration — nutrition, exercise, sleep, and stress management support
