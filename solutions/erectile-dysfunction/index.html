Understanding Erectile Dysfunction

Erectile dysfunction (ED) affects over 30 million American men. It's more than an inconvenience — it impacts confidence, relationships, and overall quality of life. At Men's Health Solutions, we approach ED with the seriousness it deserves and the discretion you expect.

Common Causes

  • Vascular issues: Reduced blood flow due to cardiovascular disease, high blood pressure, or diabetes
  • Hormonal imbalances: Low testosterone, thyroid dysfunction, or elevated estrogen
  • Neurological factors: Nerve damage from surgery, injury, or conditions like diabetes
  • Psychological factors: Stress, anxiety, depression, and performance anxiety
  • Medications: Antidepressants, blood pressure medications, and others
  • Lifestyle factors: Poor diet, lack of exercise, smoking, alcohol use, and insufficient sleep

Our Treatment Approach

We start by identifying the underlying cause through comprehensive evaluation including bloodwork, cardiovascular assessment, and detailed health history. Treatment is tailored to your specific situation:

  • Oral medications (PDE5 inhibitors)
  • Hormone optimization when hormonal imbalances are contributing
  • Lifestyle modifications — diet, exercise, sleep, and stress management
  • Combination therapies for complex cases
